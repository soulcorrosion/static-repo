                    Jimmy Choo

                    Lynn 100 sandals

                    Known for their classic designs that exude timeless elegance, Jimmy Choo presents these black calf leather, goat skin and suede Lynn 100 sandals featuring an open toe, a strappy design, a branded insole, micro stud details, a high stiletto heel and a star appliqué.

                    Designer Backstory

                    Founded in London as a bespoke atelier, Jimmy Choo is a 21st century luxury accessories brand with shoes at its heart. A pioneer in the art of celebrity dressing, it was among the first to bring shoes and handbags to Hollywood, where the red carpet proved to be the ideal runway. Since the company’s inception in 1996, Creative Director Sandra Choi has established trademark pieces such as the studded stiletto, the glitter pump and the elegant flat. The sexy cut, fashionable design and exceptional Italian craftsmanship define the iconic Jimmy Choo style.
